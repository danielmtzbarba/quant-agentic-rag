# Risk Agent

## Purpose

Surface downside drivers that could invalidate the thesis.

## Preferred Inputs

- `10-K` Item 1A Risk Factors
- `10-Q` Part II Item 1A Risk Factors
- litigation / regulatory news
- balance-sheet and cash-flow context

## Risk Taxonomy

- valuation risk
- governance risk
- balance-sheet risk
- execution risk
- regulatory or legal risk
- sentiment risk

## Output Contract

- risk category
- severity
- unresolved uncertainty
- supporting evidence ids
